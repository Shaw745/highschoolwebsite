Academic excellence, our watchword

At New Day College, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that integrates both the Nigerian National Curriculum and international educational standards. Our dedicated faculty members employ innovative teaching methods to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a love for learning among our students. We provide a wide range of subjects including Sciences, Arts, Commerce, and Vocational Studies, ensuring that each student can pursue their interests and talents to the fullest.

We believe that every child has the potential to excel academically when nurtured in the right environment. Our classrooms are structured to encourage active participation, collaborative learning, and independent research. From Junior Secondary School (JSS) through Senior Secondary School (SSS), our students are guided to develop the discipline, resilience, and confidence needed to thrive in higher education and the workplace.

Academic excellence at New Day College is not just about high grades. It is about building strong character, integrity, and a lifelong passion for knowledge. Our students are consistently challenged, supported, and celebrated as they grow into responsible global citizens.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Intensive and well-structured curriculum with a strong focus on student well-being and academic growth.
  • Passionate, highly qualified teachers who combine subject expertise with genuine care for every learner.
  • Character development, leadership training, and opportunities for creative expression through clubs and societies.
  • Regular continuous assessment, term examinations, and detailed feedback to ensure students remain on track.
  • Holistic approach to education that promotes academic excellence, personal discipline, spiritual growth, and social responsibility.
  • Small class sizes in key subjects for closer monitoring and personalised academic support where needed.

Our Academic Programme

At New Day College, we offer a rigorous curriculum designed to prepare students for national and international examinations. Our academic programme includes:

  • Junior Secondary School (JSS) curriculum.
  • Senior Secondary School (SSS) curriculum with thorough preparation for WAEC and NECO examinations.
  • Science, Arts, and Commercial streams to suit each learner’s strengths and career aspirations.
  • Continuous assessment and term examinations for steady monitoring of progress.
  • Remedial and enrichment classes for students who need extra support or additional academic challenge.

Junior Secondary (JSS)

Strong foundation in English, Mathematics, Basic Science, Business Studies, Social Studies, ICT, and more, with emphasis on study habits and core skills.

Senior Secondary (SSS)

Specialised subject combinations in Sciences, Arts, and Commerce, with structured exam coaching, past question drills, and career guidance.

Support & Enrichment

Homework clinics, holiday lessons, debate clubs, STEM projects, entrepreneurship exposure, and literacy programmes that stretch our students beyond the classroom.

Our Teaching & Learning Approach

Teaching at New Day College is interactive and student-centred. Lessons are carefully planned to blend explanation, discussion, practice, and reflection. Teachers use instructional materials, technology, real-life case studies, and hands-on activities to bring abstract concepts to life.

We encourage students to ask questions, think independently, and collaborate with their peers. Regular class presentations, group projects, and research assignments help develop communication, problem-solving, and leadership skills.

Our teachers also maintain close contact with parents and guardians through progress reports, open days, and scheduled meetings to ensure that each child receives the support they need both at school and at home.

Continuous Assessment

Weekly tests, assignments, and projects form part of our continuous assessment system, helping students stay engaged and giving teachers regular insight into their progress.

Technology in the Classroom

Where applicable, digital tools, multimedia resources, and computer-based practice are integrated to enhance understanding and expose students to modern learning environments.

Mentoring & Guidance

Class teachers and counsellors provide mentoring, helping students set goals, manage time, and prepare for life after secondary school.

What makes us outstanding

Weekend Take-Home Reading Tasks

Every weekend, our students receive carefully selected reading tasks across different subjects and genres. On Mondays, they present and discuss what they have read with their classmates and teachers.

This simple but powerful routine helps them build strong vocabulary, master key concepts, and develop confidence in public speaking. It also encourages them to read widely, think critically, and articulate their thoughts clearly in front of others.

Effective Take-Home Assignments

Our regular take-home assignments are more than routine homework. They are designed to help students apply theoretical concepts to real-life problems, keeping them engaged, challenged, and focused.

Teachers deliberately structure these tasks to develop problem-solving skills, creativity, research habits, and a sense of responsibility. Parents are also given clear guidance on how to support their children’s learning without doing the work for them.

Previous Knowledge Test (PKT)

At the beginning of each term or new topic, students write a Previous Knowledge Test. This helps teachers identify strengths, gaps, and misconceptions so that new learning builds on a firm foundation instead of guesswork. It also prepares students to think analytically and recall necessary prior knowledge.

English Language Mastery

English lessons are subdivided into reading, writing, listening, and speaking sessions. Through spelling bees, debates, vocabulary games, drama, and structured language drills, students develop fluency, confidence, and mastery of English communication.

Accountability Partner System

Students are paired or grouped as learning partners who encourage one another, review notes together, and keep each other accountable. This practice builds teamwork, discipline, healthy study habits, and shared responsibility for academic success.

Read-A-Book Project

Students participate in our Read-A-Book Project each term, where they read selected books and present their reviews. This builds a lifelong love for reading, strengthens comprehension skills, and deepens exposure to diverse ideas beyond the classroom.

Teacher Mentorship

Our teachers mentor students beyond the classroom. They listen, guide, and help students set goals, overcome challenges, and discover their strengths. This nurturing relationship helps students feel valued and supported in their academic journey.

Examinations & Academic Outcomes

Our students sit for all major external examinations including WAEC and NECO, with consistent performance that reflects the depth of our preparation and the commitment of our staff. Regular mock exams, exam clinics, and revision boot camps are organised to build confidence and sharpen exam techniques.

Many of our graduates go on to reputable universities and tertiary institutions within Nigeria and overseas. We take pride in the fact that they are not only academically prepared, but also grounded in values that help them stand out wherever they go.

At New Day College, academics is more than a timetable – it is a carefully guided journey that shapes futures.
