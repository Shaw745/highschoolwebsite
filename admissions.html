Our Admission Process

Our competitive admission process is designed to be clear, straightforward, and friendly. We support every family at each stage:

1

Step One – Purchase / Request Form

Obtain our admission form from the school office at New Day College, 5 Ayodele Awodeji St, Ikosi Ketu, Lagos, or request a digital copy via email or WhatsApp. Parents are guided on how to complete the form accurately.

2

Step Two – Entrance Assessment

Prospective students sit for written tests in English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge appropriate to their class. This helps us place each learner in the right level and identify areas where support may be needed.

3

Step Three – Interview & Offer

Successful candidates and their parents are invited for a brief interview with the school management. Once approved, an admission letter and fee schedule are issued and the enrollment process is completed.

Throughout this process, our admissions team is available to answer questions, arrange school tours, and provide guidance on transitioning from previous schools.

Entry requirements

Junior Secondary School (JSS 1 – 3)

  • Completed admission form.
  • Photocopies of last school result or transfer certificate (if applicable).
  • Two recent passport photographs.
  • Birth certificate or sworn age declaration.
  • Entrance examination and successful interview.

Senior Secondary School (SSS 1 – 3)

  • Completed admission form.
  • Junior WAEC / BECE result (for SSS 1) and most recent school report.
  • Subject combination alignment with chosen stream (Science, Arts or Commercial).
  • Two recent passport photographs.
  • Entrance assessment and interview with the principal.

School fees

Our fees are structured to reflect the quality of education, facilities and care we provide while remaining competitive within Lagos. Detailed fee schedules are shared after a successful placement, and flexible payment plans may be discussed with the accounts department.

Scholarships & discounts

In selected cases, New Day College offers partial scholarships and performance-based discounts to outstanding students, as well as consideration for siblings. These opportunities are communicated at the beginning of each session.

Guidance & support

Our admissions and counselling teams support new students as they settle into school life. Orientation programmes, mentoring, and close follow-up ensure that every learner feels welcomed, valued, and ready to succeed.
