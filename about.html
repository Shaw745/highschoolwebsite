About New Day College

A nurturing and disciplined school dedicated to raising confident learners and future leaders who thrive academically and socially.

About New Day College

New Day College is a co-educational secondary school located in Ikosi Ketu, Lagos. The college was established with a simple but powerful vision: to provide a place where young people can receive excellent education in an atmosphere of discipline, warmth, and genuine love for God and others. From our humble beginnings, New Day College has grown into a vibrant learning community that consistently nurtures students to stand out academically and morally.

We serve families who desire a balanced education for their children – one that goes beyond passing examinations to building character, work ethic, and leadership potential. Our teachers, administrative staff and management team work together as one big family, creating a school environment where every child is known by name, encouraged to ask questions, and challenged to stretch beyond their comfort zone.

Over the years, our students have distinguished themselves in external examinations, competitions, and leadership roles in their communities. Many of our graduates have gone on to reputable universities within and outside Nigeria, and they continue to make us proud by the way they live out the values they received at New Day College.

Our Vision

To be a leading Christ-centred secondary school in Nigeria that raises academically excellent, emotionally balanced and morally upright young people who will positively influence their families, professions and communities wherever they go.

Our Mission

To provide a disciplined, caring and stimulating learning environment where dedicated teachers use modern methods, sound curriculum and strong moral guidance to help every child discover their God-given abilities, develop confidence, and step into their purpose as lifelong learners and leaders.

Our Philosophy

We believe that every child is a unique gift from God, carrying within them seeds of greatness that can flourish with the right guidance, support and opportunities. For us, education is not simply the transfer of information but the total development of the child – spirit, soul and body.

Our philosophy of education is built on diligence, integrity, and excellence. We train our students to take responsibility for their choices, to respect others, and to approach every task with a strong sense of purpose. We model these values every day as staff, knowing that children learn more from who we are than from what we say.

At New Day College, we also believe that learning should be joyful. Our classrooms are spaces where students feel free to express ideas, make mistakes, and grow. Discipline is firm but fair, always aimed at correction and growth rather than fear.

Objectives of the College

  • • To raise God-fearing boys and girls who become responsible citizens with strong moral values.
  • • To deliver standard education that prepares students for higher studies within and outside Nigeria.
  • • To promote the mental, spiritual and physical well-being of our students through robust academic, co-curricular and extra-curricular programmes.
  • • To help each learner discover, refine and maximise their gifts, skills and potentials in a supportive environment.
  • • To raise trailblazers and problem-solvers who will become positive influences in their generation.

New Day Plans for Students

When a child walks through our gate, we do not only see who they are now; we see who they can become in the next five to ten years. Our programmes and routines are deliberately structured with this future in mind:

  • To see our students excel academically with strong grades in internal and external examinations.
  • To make them tech-savvy and properly exposed to the productive uses of technology in learning and communication.
  • To help every student identify their strengths – whether in sciences, arts, sports, music, leadership or entrepreneurship – and receive the right mentorship.
  • To train them to speak and write confidently, think critically, and solve problems creatively.
  • To ensure that by the time they graduate, they are ready to thrive in universities and workplaces anywhere in the world.

Our Core Values

The culture of New Day College is grounded on a set of core values that guide our daily decisions, shape our expectations and influence the way we relate with one another:

Faith

We acknowledge God as the source of wisdom and strength, and we encourage students to trust Him, live by His Word and show love to others.

Excellence

We do ordinary things in extraordinary ways, paying attention to details and constantly improving on our best efforts.

Integrity

We value honesty, transparency and doing the right thing even when no one is watching. Integrity is expected from staff and students alike.

Discipline

We maintain high standards of behaviour, punctuality and commitment to duty, while still showing grace and understanding.

Service

We teach our students to use their gifts to serve others, to show kindness and to take responsibility for the wellbeing of those around them.

Respect

We treat everyone with dignity regardless of age, background or position, and we celebrate our diversity as a strength.
