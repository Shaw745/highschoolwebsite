Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

1. What classes does New Day College run?

We run Junior Secondary School (JSS 1–3) and Senior Secondary School (SSS 1–3) with Science, Arts and Commercial options in the senior classes.

2. Do you offer day or boarding?

New Day College currently operates as a day school. However, we work closely with parents to support transportation and after school needs where possible.

3. How can I get the latest school fees information?

For up-to-date fee details, please contact the school office via phone, WhatsApp or email. Fees may vary slightly by class and session, so we prefer to confirm them directly.

4. Can I speak with a counsellor or the principal?

Yes. Parents can book an appointment to speak with the principal or guidance counsellor regarding their child’s progress, behaviour or any special need.
