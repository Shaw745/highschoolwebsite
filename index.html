About New Day College

New Day College is a distinguished secondary school located in the heart of Ikosi Ketu, Lagos. We are dedicated to providing a holistic education that emphasizes academic excellence, moral discipline and character formation.

Our mission is to develop students who are intellectually competent, emotionally balanced and socially responsible. We blend the Nigerian curriculum with modern teaching methods, rich co-curricular activities and a supportive environment that helps every learner discover their strengths.

At New Day College, we partner with parents to raise young people who can think critically, communicate clearly and live out strong values in a changing world.
